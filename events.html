Join Us at TECH FEST 2025!

April 22, 2025 | Ajasin Foundation Hall, Owo | Explore the Future of Technology

What to Expect at TECH FEST 2025

TECH FEST 2025 is an electrifying event where students, educators, and tech enthusiasts come together to explore the future of technology. Hosted by Moyacode in partnership with the Ajasin Foundation, this event will feature hands-on coding workshops, inspiring tech talks, and networking opportunities.

Date & Location

April 22, 2025 | Ajasin Foundation Hall, Owo

Tentative Agenda

  • 9:00 AM: Registration and Welcome
  • 10:00 AM: Keynote Address – “The Future of Tech”
  • 11:00 AM: Coding Workshops (Scratch, HTML/CSS, JavaScript)
  • 1:00 PM: Lunch Break
  • 2:00 PM: Tech Talks and Panel Discussion
  • 4:00 PM: Networking and Closing Remarks
