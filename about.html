About Moyacode

Empowering the next generation of coders through education and innovation.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We inspire and empower the next generation of coders through high-quality coding education that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Vision

To make coding education accessible to all, especially underprivileged students, and build a community of innovators in Ondo State and beyond.

Our Story

Moyacode was founded in 2024 by [Your Name], a passionate educator and coder, to bridge the gap in coding education for students in Ondo State. We believe coding is a superpower that can unlock endless opportunities, and we’re here to make it accessible to everyone through hands-on learning with tools like Scratch, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
